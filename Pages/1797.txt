Undergraduate Studies











Whether you are a high school senior, a student hoping to transfer to UIC, or a college graduate pursuing an advanced degree, the Department of Mechanical and Industrial Engineering’s faculty and staff are here to assist you! Below you will find information regarding our undergraduate programs. Best wishes on your academic endeavors and we hope to see you at the UIC College of Engineering.
MIE offers -accredited undergraduate degrees in Mechanical Engineering (ME), Industrial Engineering (IE) and Engineering Management (EM).
In each program, the department focuses on providing fundamental and advanced courses that prepare students for careers in the engineering profession, or to move on to advanced study at the graduate level.

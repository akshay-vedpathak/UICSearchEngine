OIS News - 2/22/2018


xml version="1.0" encoding="utf-16"?USCIS Expands Credit Card Payment Option to Most Fee-Based FormsEffective
Feb. 14, 2018, U.S. Citizenship and Immigration Services (USCIS) announced that
applicants will now be able to pay filing fees on 41
fee-based forms processed at USCIS
Lockbox facilities via credit card (Visa, MasterCard, American
Express or Discover).
 
More
information about this announcement can be found on the USCIS
website. Members of the UIC international community who are in the
process of submitting fee-based applications to USCIS are encouraged to work
with Office of International Services Advisors to understand and properly take
advantage of this new USCIS fee payment option.
 
Questions? Feel
free to contact
the Office of International Services.

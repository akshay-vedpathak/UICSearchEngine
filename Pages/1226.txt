          Learning Technology Solutions        




Category: , , Audience: , Overview Formed in 2016, the Learning Technology Solutions (LTS) team brings together the instructional design and software support aspects of the Instructional Technology Lab, with the classroom and computer lab technology support of the Learning Environments & Technology Services office. This group works to improve your experience as an instructor here at UIC by providing a central point of contact for all of your learning technology needs.
OUR MISSION
To enhance student success and faculty empowerment through learning technologies.
Features We fulfill our mission by providing and supporting the following services, and offering solutions to the learning technology challenges faced by Faculty and the UIC community at large.
Learning Tools
The LTS team not only provides and supports the , , ,  and many other tools for the UIC community, it can help identify the best combination of these tools to meet your specific instructional needs.  Here are just some of the tools we support:

Learning Environments
The LTS team provides a single point of contact for support of all aspects of the .  We not only assist with the use of technology in these spaces, we also actively monitor the spaces for any facility-related problems and work on your behalf with Facilities Management, Classroom Scheduling, and other groups on campus so you can focus on the teaching and learning experience!
Course Design & Development
The instructional design staff of LTS offers support for the use of quality design standards and best practices in course design for technology-enhanced, blended, and fully online courses. This includes the exploration of collaborative tools, the redesign of courses to meet accessibility requirements, and the improvement of the course structure in general.
Equipment Lending
LTS offers equipment for short-term loan to both faculty and students
Faculty can check out equipment including microphones, laptops, and presentation remotes for use in a specific class session from our LTS Support office in Lecture Center E room E112
Students can check out laptops and mobile device charging cables for a three-day loan from the BSB Learning Center
Both faculty and students can check out laptops, camcorders, audio recorders, and more from the LTS Multimedia Lab in the Daley Library IDEA Commons.
Professional Development
The LTS team provides  in various formats. We offer webinars and workshops on a variety of topics including all of the basic instructional tools supported at UIC. The goal of these sessions is to help instructors improve their use of technology by providing guidance on the use of best practices for teaching with technology. We also design tailored sessions for academic departments. Online video tutorials are also available on a wide range of topics through our Lynda.com subscription.
Community Engagement
We continually strive to create a campus community that shares best practices for using technology for teaching and learning.

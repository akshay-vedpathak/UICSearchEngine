ITA English Development Courses

 









The ITA Program at UIC currently offers 3 courses to help international students improve their oral communication, interaction competency, and teaching skills.
 is designed for current or prospective international teaching assistants who want to improve their English pronunciation. Students work on self-monitoring strategies to improve pronunciation from the sound-level, sentence-level, and discourse-level.

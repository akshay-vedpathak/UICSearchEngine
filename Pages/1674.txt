Motor Row & the Auto Show






 
 


Join the UIC College of Engineering on Saturday, February 17, 2018 for brunch at Motor Row Brewing and an outing at the Chicago Auto Show. Connect with UIC Engineering alumni and meet UIC Motorsports—the College’s Society of Automotive Engineers—whose award-winning formula and baja cars will be on display for the sixth year in a row. Tickets are $25, which include brunch and admission to the Chicago Auto Show.
10:30 a.m. Enjoy Brunch at Motor Row Brewing (2337 South Michigan Avenue, Chicago IL 60616)12:00 p.m. Meet UIC Motorsports at McCormick Place (2301 South King Drive, Chicago, IL 60616)12:30 p.m. Explore the Chicago Auto Show
Suggested Parking: Southwest corner of McCormick Place Parking Lot A. Rate is $23 for up to 16 hours. The Chicago Auto Show is a 10-minute walk from Motor Row Brewing.
RSVP by Friday, February 9 and find more details .
For more information, please contact Maurice Willis, Associate Director of Engagement & Participation, at  or (312) 996-9422.

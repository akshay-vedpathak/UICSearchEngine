Bring your group to campus 

UIC Admissions offers tours for college and career readiness groups that want to learn more about UIC and the benefits of higher education. Group visits typically consist of an information session and a student-led walking tour of campus. 
Expect to stay around 1.5 hours. Register soon, spaces fill up quickly.

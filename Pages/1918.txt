Bioengineering at BMES











Highlight from UIC Bioengineering at the annual Biomedical Engineering Society meeting.





2018 BMES Annual Meeting #BIOEatBMES2018











Four UIC Bioengineering Faculty (current and former) now serving as department heads of BME/BioE programs across the country, met at the Council of Chairs dinner at the annual meeting of the Biomedical Engineering Society in Atlanta, GA on October 17, 2018. From left to right: Tom Royston (UIC), Bin He (Carnegie Mellon University), Tejal Desai (University of California at San Francisco), and Mike Cho (University of Texas at Arlington).

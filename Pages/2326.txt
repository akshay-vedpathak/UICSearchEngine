International Student Career WeekGet a jump start on your career in the United States during
International Student Career Week. Each spring the Office of
International Services collaborates with campus partners to host a
series of career-themed events focused on the specific needs and perspectives of international students.
 

Join the Office of International Services and campus
partners for International Student Career Week 2018! Registration is required
for most events and seating is limited. Please see each individual event
listing for details. 

Monday, March 12 
Improv for Networking
12:00-1:00 p.m. | Student Services
Building, 1200 West Harrison, Conference Room A
Would you like to improve your communication
and networking skills? Do you like to laugh? In this workshop, UIC Lecturer and
Second City Instructor Ranjit Souri will teach you some improvisational comedy
basics that you can use to enhance your networking skills!
To register - Complete the online registration form by
Friday, March 9, 12:00 noon.
 
Navigating a Career Fair
4:00-6:00 p.m. | Student Center East,
750 South Halsted Street, Room 713
Join UIC Career Services Associate Director for Employer
Relations, Jaime Velasquez, as he explains how to maximize your time at a
career fair. Special considerations for international students will also be
discussed.
To register - Complete the online registration form by
Friday, March 9, 12:00 noon.


Tuesday, March 13
Managing
Multiple Offers
12:00-1:00 p.m. | Student Services
Building, 1200 West Harrison, Conference Room A
What do you do when you receive, or
expect to receive, more than one job offer at once? You may be surprised to
learn that there is definitely a right and a wrong way to handle this
situation. Patrick Devanney, Associate Director of the Engineering Career
Center, will discuss how to navigate this tricky but common scenario with
professionalism. 
To register - Complete the online registration form by
Monday, March 12, 12:00 noon.
 
Etiquette
Dinner
5:00-8:00 p.m. | Student Center East,
750 South Halsted Street, Illinois Room
Join the Office of International Services
and Career Services for a unique evening of dinner and learning. Participants
will be served a multi-course meal accompanied by U.S. business dinner
etiquette instruction. To compliment the immersion experience, business casual
attire is strongly encouraged. 
To register - Visit the Office of
International Services to pay a $5 participation fee (cash only) by Tuesday,
March 6, 5:00 p.m. 
 
Wednesday, March 14
International
Alumni Career Panel
4:30-7:30 p.m. | Student Services
Building, 1200 West Harrison, Conference Room BC
Hear real-life job search and career
experiences from recent international UIC alumni working in the U.S. The
Q&A panel will be followed by a networking practice session. Light
refreshments will be served. This event is co-hosted by the Engineering Career
Center, Career Services, Graduate Student Council, and the Office of
International Services.
To register - Complete the online registration form by
Tuesday, March 13, 12:00 noon.


Thursday, March 15
All Majors
Career Fair
10:00-2:00 p.m. | UIC Forum, 725 West
Roosevelt Road
Attend UIC’s largest career fair
featuring over 100 employers from various industries including technology,
engineering, government, business, healthcare & pharmaceuticals, and
non-profit. This event is organized by Career Services and will feature Pop-Up
CPT & OPT Advising by the Office of International Services.

More
information - Visit the Career
Services website.


Friday, March 16
Forecasting
the Employment Immigration Landscape
12:00-1:00 p.m. | Student Services
Building, 1200 West Harrison, Conference Room BC
In the current political climate it can
be overwhelming to think about entering the U.S. workforce as an international
student in need of visa sponsorship. Hear from UIC Associate Vice Provost for
Global Engagement and Executive Director of the Office of International
Services, Jim Hammerschmidt, PhD, as he broadly discusses the current
immigration employment landscape and what changes, if any, can be expected in
the near future. 
To register - Complete
the online registration form by
Thursday, March 15, 12:00 noon.

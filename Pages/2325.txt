International Education WeekInternational Education Week is an annual, worldwide celebration of international education and exchange sponsored by the U.S. Department of State’s Bureau of Educational and Cultural Affairs. Each year, units within the Office of Global Engagement collaborate to feature the international events, programs and initiatives that occur at UIC. See below for information on this year’s events and visit the Department of State’s IEW website for a listing of events worldwide. 
  
International Education Week 2018
Monday, Nov. 12 to Friday, Nov. 16
For a full list of IEW events at UIC, please visit the Study Abroad Office website.

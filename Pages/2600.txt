Center for the Advancement of Teaching-Learning Communities 

 








The UIC Center for the Advancement of Teaching-Learning Communities (TLC) strives to promote and develop the growth of faculty as teachers of UIC’s diverse student body, strengthening UIC’s culture of faculty commitment to student success. Through the development of programs, policies, resources and rewards, TLC strives to encourage every member of the academic community to develop interests, abilities, and achievements as both teachers and learners.

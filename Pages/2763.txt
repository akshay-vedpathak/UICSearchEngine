STEM Optional Practical Training (OPT) Extension Webinar 
        









Date and Time 
        




Dec 14 2018 3:00 PM -- 4:00 PM









Location 
        

Online Webinar




Description


F-1 students interested in applying for STEM Optional Practical Training (OPT) Extension are invited to attend an introductory STEM OPT Extension Webinar. The STEM OPT Extension webinar covers essential information about the benefit such as eligibility requirements, application process, and timeline. Students will leave with an understanding of how and when to apply for STEM OPT Extension along with the necessary application materials. Students may attend a STEM OPT Extension Webinar anytime during their approved 12 Month OPT authorization. Registration for the webinar is required.

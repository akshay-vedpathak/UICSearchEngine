EXPERIENCE UIC ENGINEERING IN PERSON

The best way for you to discover  the College of Engineering at UIC is to come see for yourself.
Our college offers a number of options for campus visits. We also offer scheduled preview days and opens house for freshmen and transfer students. We’ll do everything we can to accommodate you.
 
Please contact our academic resource center to talk to one of our advisors and to schedule a visit. We hope to see you soon!

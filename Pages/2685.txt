Outreach



The ESP IGERT program has established collaborations with several international partners and industry. Our students have the opportunity to participate in one summer research internship at the Politecnico di Milano, Italy or the National University of Singapore and in one summer internship with our industry partners.Our students will also be involved in one project reaching to high school students and teachers in the Chicago area. This project will involve training students and teachers in the issues of ESP.

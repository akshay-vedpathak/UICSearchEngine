F-1/OPT,
CAP GAP,
STEM

• I-20, I-94
            • EAD Card
• Passport

• Remain involved in work   related to your degree
            • Report any change in   residential address, program extensions, changes in employer, departure, etc. to your I-20 sponsor ASAP
• Study may be permitted –   contact your I-20 sponsor



J-1 Academic Training
• DS-2019, I-94
            • Passport
• DS-2019 sponsor’s   “Academic Training Letter”

• Remain involved in research or   teaching  activities related to your program goals/field as stated on your  DS-2019• Report any change in   residential address to your DS-2019  sponsor within 10 business days
            • Maintain adequate J health   insurance
• Study is permitted



J-1 3rd Party 
(DS-2019 not issued by UIC) 

• DS-2019, I-94
            • Passport

• Remain involved in research or   teaching  activities related to your program goals/field as stated on your  DS-2019• Update your sponsor with any   changes to your program &/or  address
            • Maintain adequate J health   insurance
• Study is permitted



J-2
• DS-2019, I-94
            • Passport
• EAD Card,
if employed 

• Do not work without USCIS work   authorization
            • Maintain adequate J health   insurance
• Study is permitted
• Extensions dependent upon J-1’s   program end date



B-1/B-2
• I-94
            • Passport

• Remain involved in the   activity for which the department invited you
            • May be reimbursed for   expenses (airfare, insurance, per diem, etc.)
• May act as a consultant   for up to 9 days, but cannot legally  accept teaching or   research positions, or other paid employment
• Extensions may be possible if   a timely request is filed with  USCIS via your self-completed I-539 application and supporting evidence
• Study is not permitted



WB/WT
• I-94
            • Passport

• May be reimbursed for   expenses (airfare, insurance, per diem, etc.)
            • May act as a consultant   for up to 9 days, but cannot legally  accept teaching or   research positions, or other paid employment
• No extensions are possible –   90 days maximum.
• Study is not permitted

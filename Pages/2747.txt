Spring 2019 International Student Orientation






            

 
Schedule of Events
Sweet Home Chicago
Wednesday, January 9 | 11:30 a.m. - 4:30 pm
Student Center East, 750 South Halsted Street, Illinois Room
Get to know your new home, Chicago! This session includes a presentation on the history and culture of Chicago followed by a guided tour of the city in a classic, open-air trolley. Get your camera ready for some fantastic photo opportunities! Lunch and a resource fair are included. 

This event is strongly encouraged but  is required for participation.
New
International Student Summit
Friday, January 11 | 8:30 a.m. - 4:00
p.m.
Student Center East, 750 South Halsted Street, Illinois Room
The New International Student Summit brings all incoming international students starting their programs in spring together for one exciting day. Information covered during this session includes immigration responsibilities, U.S. and classroom culture, and essential UIC resources. The day will conclude with a Q&A panel with UIC student Global Ambassadors. Breakfast and lunch are included. 

This event is mandatory for all incoming international students;
please plan your travel accordingly.  is required for
participation.
Gurnee Mills Shopping Trip
Saturday, January 19 | 9:45 a.m. - 5:00
p.m. 
Student Services Building, 1200 West Harrison Street to Gurnee, IL
Join OIS on a trip to the largest outlet and full-price shopping destination in Illinois.  is a shopping mall with over 200 stores. Students typically buy household items and winter clothing. OIS will provide transportation from the UIC campus to the mall in Gurnee, IL and back.
This session is optional but in-person registration is required for participation. Please visit the Office of International Services upon your arrival in Chicago to register.

 
Registration & Fee Information
Registration is required for all
mandatory and optional International Student Orientation events. Please
register using the . 
All incoming international
students are charged a non-refundable Orientation/Arrival Fee of $149 to their
university bill to cover the costs of initial arrival services and programs,
regardless of ISO participation. However, there is no additional charge for participation
in selected ISO events.


Guest Information
Family and guests are welcome at
all International Student Orientation events. There is a $10 per person fee for
each ISO event that a guest attends. This Orientation Event Fee will be charged
to your university bill at the beginning of your first semester of study. You
may register your guests when completing the  form.


Countdown to Chicago Pre-Arrival Communications
Countdown to Chicago emails
are sent to incoming international students in the weeks prior to their arrival in Chicago. They highlight a variety of pre-arrival preparation topics including International Student Orientation,
registering for classes, housing in Chicago, and more. 








Questions?
Questions regarding Spring 2019 International Student Orientation can be directed to the Office of
International Services at newintl@uic.edu or by phone at +1 (312) 996-3121.

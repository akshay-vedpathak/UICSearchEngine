Dear Honors students,
The Honors College Diversity Committee and the Honors Ambassadors are excited to welcome you to a De-Stress Zone for finals week! Whether you are taking a study break or you want to de-stress in between exams, join us in BH 121 (Quiet Study) from 8am to 5pm during finals week. There will be activities (e.g., self-soothe kits, coloring, letters to self, and games). We will also provide healthy and indulgent snacks!

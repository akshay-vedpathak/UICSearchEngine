Diversity Program Participation Waiver
The Diversity Program Participation Waiver is designed to expand participation of underrepresented students in graduate education, intending to pursue an academic and/or research career.
To be eligible for this application fee waiver, you must meet the following criteria: 

Be applying for admission to a Ph.D. program


Have completed a bachelor's degree by the August preceding enrollment in the graduate program


Have a cumulative undergraduate GPA of 3.0 or higher (4.0 scale)


Be a citizen or a permanent resident of the U.S.


Belong to a traditionally underrepresented group in the chosen field of study


Have participated in one of the following programs:

Amgen Scholars Program


California State University Sally Casanova Pre-Doctoral Scholarship Program


Gates Millennium Scholarship Program


GEM Fellowship Program 


Howard Hughes


Louis Stokes Alliance for Minority Participation


Mellon Mays Undergraduate Fellowship Program 


Minority Access to Research Careers (MARC) Scholars Program


Minority Biomedical Research Support (MBRS)


Morehouse College Hopps Scholars Program


National Name Exchange (NNE)


NIH (MARC, RISE, or PREP)


NIMH-COR 


NSF (LSAMP or REU)


PhD Project


Project 1000


The Leadership Alliance


TRIO Student Support Services (SSS) Program


UIC Pathways to Success


UIC Summer Research Opportunities Program (SROP)


University Maryland-Baltimore County Meyerhoff Program


In order to be considered for this waiver, please select “Diversity Program Participation Waiver” from the waiver dropdown on the Fee Assessment page at the end of the graduate application. Note that a letter drafted and signed by the appropriate Program Director would need to be obtained and uploaded into the application to verify participation.

Financial Aid Steps
The links on the left will guide you through the aid process, from filing your Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA) to receiving your aid and maintaining your eligibility.
You must go through the aid application process every year.
The FAFSA is available October 1st every year. We recommend that you complete your FAFSA prior to our priority deadline of February 15th each year.

National Center on Faculty Development and Diversity (NCFDD)

 








UIC is proud to announce an institutional membership in the National Center on Faculty Development and Diversity (NCFDD), a mentoring organization for faculty members, postdocs, and graduate students designed to address a range of topics relevant to your professional development and success. In addition to programing that is relevant to all, this organization also offers a programmatic emphasis on topics that are of unique interest to underrepresented individuals.
Information on how to register for the wealth of resources that are freely available to you through this program .
Charu Thakral, Interim Associate Chancellor and Vice Provost for Diversity

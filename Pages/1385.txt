Summer Financial Aid
There is no additional application for summer; all degree-seeking students with a 2018-2019 FAFSA who enroll in summer courses will be considered. All summer sessions count as one term.

Undergraduate/Graduate students need at least 5 credit hours for summer aid
Professional students need at least 6 credit hours for summer aid

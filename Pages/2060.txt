Two 2018 Graduates Awarded Fulbright Grants


Posted on August 22, 2018


















Sanjana Srinivasan and Kajal Sachdev, graduates of the Class of 2018, have been awarded Fulbright Grants. Both were students in the GPPA (Guaranteed Professional Program Admission) Medicine Program and plan to start medical school after completing Fulbright supported research.
Srinivasan is relocating to Pamplona, Spain for nine months to conduct research at the University of Navarra.
Sachdev is headed to Maastricht University in the Netherlands to complete a master's degree in legal psychology.

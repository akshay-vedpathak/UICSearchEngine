Tags: , , Service: , , To access many authenticated university services and create accounts, new students, faculty and staff must first . The following information is required to complete the activation:

Activation Token (sent to admitted students and provided to departmentally-sponsored affiliates)
If you have this information, you can  now. During the activation process you will have the opportunity to:
register for UIC Alert, an optional free emergency notification system
set your password, used to access many  as well as most authenticated University of Illinois computing and networking services
register password recovery options for self-service management of your password
set the forwarding of your NetID@uic.edu email address

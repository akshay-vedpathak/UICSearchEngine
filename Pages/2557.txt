Services for Students with Disabilities

 








The  works to ensure the accessibility of UIC programs, classes, and services to students with disabilities. Services are available for students who have documented disabilities, including vision or hearing impairments and emotional or physical disabilities. Students with disability/access needs or questions may contact the Office of Disability Services at (312) 413-2183 (voice) or (312) 413-0123 (TTY only).

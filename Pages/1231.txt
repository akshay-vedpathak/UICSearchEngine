ACCC Annual Survey
The Academic Computing & Communications Center (ACCC) Annual Survey is our primary means of obtaining information and feedback from the UIC community. Survey results are collected and analyzed to identify the most frequently used ACCC services, tech support quality ratings, and current academic technology-related needs.

ACCC Annual Report
This is the annual report published by ACCC. The report provides an overview of the accomplishments in the last year as well as the initiatives planned for the next year. The report is based on the fiscal year of the university which begins on July 1st and ends on June 30th the following year.

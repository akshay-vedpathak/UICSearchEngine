J-1 Scholar Orientation 
        









Date and Time 
        




Dec 12 2018 2:00 PM -- 3:30 PM









Location 
        

Student Services Building, 1200 West Harrison Street, Conference Room A




Description


J-1 Scholar Orientation is required for all new
Exchange Visitors. We will review the benefits and limitations of your
immigration status; travel and visa procedures; and adjusting to life and
customs in the U.S. while highlighting campus and city resources. You will be
invited to J-1 Scholar Orientation when you 
with OIS upon arrival to the U.S.

FERPA
Pursuant to the Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act (FERPA) of 1974, the University cannot disclose personally identifiable information contained in the student's education records without the student's written consent. A parent does not automatically have the right to view his/her child's records without the express written/electronic consent of the student, unless that parent can provide proof that the student is still a dependent for income tax purposes.
